Lasagna

Ingrediants

  • Italian sausage and ground beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Can of crushed tomatoes, two cans tomato sauce, two cans of tomato paste
  • Two tblsp of white-sugar
  • fresh parsley, dried basil leaves,salt,italian seasoning,fennel seeds, black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • single egg

Recipe

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving
