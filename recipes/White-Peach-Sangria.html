White Peach Sangria

Ingrediants

  • 750ml dry white wine
  • 3/4 cup peach flavored vodka
  • 6 tblsp frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 pound white peaches, pitted and sliced
  • 3/4 cup seedless red grapes, halved
  • 3/4 cup seedless green grapes, halved

Recipe

  1. Combine white wine, preach vodka,lemonade concentrade, and sugar in large pitchuer; stir until sugar is disslved, add sliced peaches, red grapes, and green grapes.
  2. Refrigerate sangria until well chilled and flavors have blended, at least 2 hours to overnight.
  3. Serve over ice, and use a slotted spoon to include sliced peaches and grapes with each serving
